Beverage

Jal Jeera

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp roasted cumin seeds (jeera) Ingredients 🌱
  • 1 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 🧂 1 tsp black salt
  • 1 tsp chaat masala
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 🍋 1–2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2–3 cups cold water
  • 🧊 Ice cubes
  • 1–2 tbsp tamarind pulp (optional but tasty)

Recipe

  • Grind mint leaves + roasted cumin + little water into a smooth paste.
  • In a large jug, add:cumin-mint paste, black salt, chaat masala, black pepper, tamarind pulp (optional).
  • Pour cold water and mix well.
  • Add lemon juice and stir.
    Adjust salt/spice as per taste.
  • Add ice cubes and serve chilled 🧊

Starter

Pav Bhaji

Ingredients:

  • 🧈2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 🧅 1 cup chopped onions
  • 🍅 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 🥔 1 cup boiled potatoes
  • 🥦 ½ cup cauliflower
  • ½ cup green peas
  • 🫑 ½ cup chopped capsicum
  • 2–3 tbsp pav bhaji masala
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • 🧂 Salt to taste
  • 🧄 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1–2 cups water
  • 🍋 Lemon juice (as needed)
  • 🌿 Fresh coriander (for garnish)
  • 🍞 6–8 pav buns
  • 🧈 Butter for roasting

Recipe

  • 🥔 Boil potatoes, cauliflower & peas until soft. Mash them well.
  • 🧈 Heat butter + oil in a pan. Add onions and sauté until golden.
  • 🧄 Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  • 🍅 Add tomatoes and cook until soft & mushy.
  • 🫑 Add capsicum and cook for 2–3 minutes.
  • Add pav bhaji masala, chilli powder & salt. Mix well.
  • 🥕 Add boiled veggies and mash everything together.
  • 💧 Add water and simmer for 10–15 minutes until thick.
  • 🧈 Finish with butter, 🍋 lemon juice, and 🌿 coriander.
  • 🔪 Slice pav buns
  • 🧈 Heat butter on tawa/pan
  • 🍞 Toast pav until golden & crispy

Main Course

Dum Aloo

Ingredients:

  • Baby potatoes 🥔
  • Onion 🧅
  • Tomato 🍅
  • Yogurt (optional) 🥛
  • Ginger-garlic paste 🧄
  • Spices: turmeric, chili, coriander, garam masala 🌶️

Recipe

  • Boil & lightly fry potatoes until golden 🥔✨
  • Sauté onion + ginger-garlic 🧅🧄
  • Add tomato puree + spices 🍅🌶️
  • Add yogurt (optional) 🥛
  • Add potatoes + water, simmer on low heat 🍲
  • Finish with garam masala + coriander 🌿

🍽️ Serve with:

  • Naan
  • Roti
  • rice

Dessert

Rabri

Ingredients:

  • Full cream milk 🥛
  • Sugar 🍬
  • Cardamom powder 🌿
  • Saffron strands 🌸
  • Chopped almonds & pistachios 🥜
  • Rose water (optional) 🌹

Recipe

  • Pour milk into a heavy pan and bring it to a boil 🥛🔥
  • Reduce heat and keep stirring, scraping cream (malai) from sides 🍶
  • Let milk slowly thicken and reduce to 1/3 of original quantity ⏳
  • Add sugar and cardamom 🌿🍬
  • Add saffron soaked in warm milk 🌸
  • Mix in nuts and simmer for a few more minutes 🥜
  • Chill or serve warm ❄️🔥
